About The Studio

We build strategic games with identity and staying power.

Big Three Games is an independent team focused on expressive worlds, replayable systems, and communities that help shape each release over time.

Our Story

Small Team, Long Vision

Independent By Design

We are a self-driven studio. That means we can move fast, test ideas, and stay close to the players who care about our games.

Systems With Character

We do not separate mechanics from mood. Every system should carry personality, visual clarity, and meaningful player decisions.

Community Feedback Loop

We release updates, read feedback, and iterate openly. Better balance and stronger progression come from that ongoing dialogue.

Built For The Long Run

Our roadmap focuses on durability: systems that remain interesting, content that expands, and worlds players want to return to.
